Our Story

Founded in 2014 by automotive enthusiast Danny Ngatia, Car Shop Kenya began as a small family business with a simple mission: to provide quality, affordable vehicles to the people of Kenya while delivering exceptional customer service.

What started with just 5 cars in a small lot in Westlands has grown into one of Nairobi's most trusted car dealerships, with over 100 vehicles in inventory and thousands of satisfied customers across Kenya.

Today, we continue to uphold our founding principles: transparency, quality, and putting our customers first in every transaction.
